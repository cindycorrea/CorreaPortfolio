ADDICTIONS

I have found the topic of addictions to be one of the most interesting that I studied. I have never struggled with an alcohol or drug addiction, so my initial thought was that this topic doesn’t apply to me much, but there are so many other types of addictions and poor habits that we just can’t seem to let go of. Here are a few I came up with while studying: playing the victim, over indulging in food, ungratefulness, choosing to be miserable, anger, lack of humility, and bringing attention to ourselves. I feel that a few of these apply to me. An addiction is not just substance abuse, it is giving into the natural man in ways that harm me and others. I need to identify these behaviors and use the power of the Atonement to rid them from my life.
