Capstone Project

Cindy Correa

Welcome to the accumulation of my Capstone Project for Teachings of Living Prophets. This project was designed to "help [me] live faithfully as I walk [my] path in these complex and difficult times." (Project Instructions) This project is broken up into three sections: Learn, Listen, Walk.

Learn

The first section of the project focused on the topics I was going to study. I was assigned the following four topics: Family, Atonement, Agency, and Addictions. I was given the task of choosing four more topics based on what I felt I needed most in my life. The topics I chose were: Gratitude, Joy in Motherhood, Service, and Let God Prevail.

Listen

I had the pleasure of researching ten quotes for each of my eight topics. These quotes can be accessed through the menu navigation by topic. After each quote, you will find a convenient link to each of the talks where I found my quotes. This was my favorite part of the project. I loved going back and listening to the apostles and prophets lead and guide us through the years.

Walk

In the last section of the project, I was to choose one of my topics to apply to my life. I developed a plan to implement it and faithfully executed it. The rest of this page is the report from this phase of the project.

Let God Prevail

Over the course of the last six weeks I have strived to let God have a greater impact on my life.
