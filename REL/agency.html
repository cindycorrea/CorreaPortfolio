AGENCY

Agency is one of the many ways that God shows His love for me. He gave me agency so that I could choose who I want to be, what I want to do, and who I want to be with. He believes in me, he trusts me, and he wants me to return to Him because I chose to. God knew that I would not always make the choices he would have me make, but he has let me go through my life how I want to. I make mistakes every day, but with my agency, I can choose to try harder, be better, and get back on the path toward Him.

Quotes

Russell M. Nelson, April 2021 General Conference, "The Temple and Your Spiritual Foundation"

“We all have our agency… We can choose to let God prevail in our lives, or not. We can choose to let God be the most powerful influence in our lives, or not.”

“Our Heavenly Father's goal in parenting is not to have His children do what is right; it is to have His children choose to do what is right and ultimately become like Him.”
